Slide 1 // Problem Statement

A Silent Crisis: Delayed Rescue Fatalities

Every year, traffic accidents cause millions of preventable deaths due to delayed accident notification and missing the critical "Golden Hour".

  • 12.0 Million Deaths: The cumulative global road fatality count over the past 10 years (average 1.2M deaths annually, WHO).
  • 4.5 Million Lives Lost: The number of fatalities directly attributed to delayed medical response over the last 10 years (approx 38% of all road deaths).
  • 35-45 Minutes Delay: Average notification time on unmonitored road corridors (e.g. Al-Anbar Expressway 1) before local authorities are alerted.
  • Under 5 Seconds: Automated AI notification response, instantly alerting emergency dispatch and preventing up to **1.5 Million deaths** per decade.

Slide 3 // Custom Dataset & Augmentation

Custom Roboflow Dataset Pipeline

Creating a robust deep-learning model requires diverse datasets representing regional hazards (e.g. Al-Anbar Expressway and Fallujah roads).

  • Custom Image Sourcing: Sourced under Muath's curation specifically targeting highway crashes, dusty weather, and vehicle fires.
  • 15,000 Augmented Images: Expanded the raw dataset via custom augmentations (contrast shifting, rotations, noise injection, and dust storm glare simulation).
  • 4 Main Classes: Labeled with precise anchors for: Accident, Fire, Motorcycle, and Vehicle.
  • Training Script (train.py): Multi-epoch GPU training of YOLOv10x producing custom weights (`best.pt`) optimized for edge devices.

Slide 6 // Vision & Tracking

Multi-Object Tracking & GMC

A dynamic bounding-box framework tracking fast vehicle objects and smoothing coordinates under severe external camera shake.

  • Custom Weights (best.pt): Specially trained custom YOLOv10 weights to predict vehicle positions in dust storms.
  • BoT-SORT Tracker: Maintains unique vehicle track IDs over long periods, handling occlusions and overlaps.
  • Global Motion Compensation: Optical Flow GMC vectors are subtracted from coordinates to prevent camera shaking false alarms.
Slide 7 // Kinematics & Physics Engine

Kalman Filters & Anomaly Trackers

Estimates vehicle trajectory dynamics to identify sharp turns, sudden decelerations, and high-risk time-to-collision margins.

  • Kalman Coordinate Smoothing: Filters out tracking noise and updates velocity matrices.
  • Time-to-Collision (TTC): Kinematic equations alert when impact horizon falls below 0.6s bounds.
  • Mahalanobis Anomaly: Computes innovation covariance matrices to identify sudden lateral lane changes.
Slide 8 // Bayesian Risk Fusion

Multi-Signal Decision Fusion

Probabilistic fusion of 11 distinct kinematic, overlap, and deep-learning warnings to confirm collisions at a strict 60% threshold.

  • Likelihood Ratios: Updates prior odds based on overlap IoU, contact logs, TTC limits, and decelerations.
  • State Machine Flow: Manages state progression for object instances.
  • 60% Decision Boundary: Standardized via BAYES_THRESH = 0.6 in config file to trigger alarms.
